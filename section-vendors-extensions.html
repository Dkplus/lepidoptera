4.2.1 Breadcrumb in List

Breadcrumbs cannot be used within an li because they are block-elements (and therefore displayed one below the other) and with too much space between them.

Source: vendors-extensions/_materialize.scss, line 116

4.2.2 Intention after fixed SideNav

A fixed SideNav hides content at the left. To prevent this, the nav and the main content (use .container or .main) will be automatically intended if it is placed after the fixed navbar.

Source: vendors-extensions/_materialize.scss, line 12

4.2.3 Screen margins

The Material Design Guidelines mention a screen edge left and right margin of 16dp.

This has been added for .main. Within the .nav-wrapper this must be applied to each element on its on because rows already have this.
